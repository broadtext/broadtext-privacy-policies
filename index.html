Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 4, 2025

BroadText is committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

The only purpose of BroadText is to help users send SMS messages to multiple recipients easily. All data (including messages and phone numbers) is handled locally on your device. We do not track, transmit, or analyze any of your personal information.

The app requires permission to access contacts and send SMS in order to function, but these permissions are only used for user-selected actions and are never misused or stored.
