Privacy Policy for BroadText

Effective Date: April 4, 2025

BroadText respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users.

Data Collection

BroadText does not collect any personal data. It only uses your device's contact and SMS functionality to send messages that you initiate manually. No information is stored or transmitted externally.

Permissions

BroadText requests the following permissions solely to function properly:

  • SEND_SMS: To send text messages on your behalf.
  • READ_CONTACTS: To allow you to choose recipients from your contact list.

Third-Party Access

No data is shared with any third-party services. The app operates completely offline and does not connect to external servers.

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page.
